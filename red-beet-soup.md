This soup is kinda good, but mostly you make it because of its cool color.

## Ingredients
- red beets
- potatoes
- vegetable broth
- onion(s)
- bay leaves
- (olive) oil
- salt
- pepper

### Optional
- unsweetened yogurt or sour cream
- cheese to grate

## Equipment
- hand blender

## Steps
- chop the onion finely and saut√© it in some oil inside a tall pot
- dice the red beets and the potatoes

- add the vegetables to the pot and stir for about 5 minutes
- warm the broth up

- pour the broth into the pot and bring it to a boil. Let everything cook for at least 15-20 minutes, until both the beets and the potatoes are soft
- grate a generous amount of cheese

- turn off the stove and blend everything together
- season with salt, pepper and optionally some extra oil and a bit of yogurt or sour cream
